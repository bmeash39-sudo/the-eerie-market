📖 THE LORE OF UMBRA

"Where the veil between worlds wears thin... and monsters shop."

🌍 THE SIX REALMS

🏰 The Crimson Courts

The realm of old money and older blood. Vampires with aristocratic lineages hold court in marble halls lit by eternal candles. Status is measured in centuries owned and coats of arms collected. Most would never be caught dead shopping at a public market—unless it's exclusive.

🌕 The Howling Moors

Wild, untamed forests where the werewolves roam. The moon never fully sets here. Fur and freedom are valued above all. It's rough, it's raw, and the local grooming salon is the only place to get a proper trim between transformations.

🍄 The Witch's Wilds

A chaotic realm where magic grows like weeds. Witches brew potions, hexes, and curses—sometimes by accident. The air smells of mushrooms, moonflower, and slightly burned experiments. No two days are the same, and the local deliveries are made by enchanted familiars.

👻 The Faded Veil

The thinnest place between life and death. Ghosts drift through walls, regrets manifest as property, and the rent is cheap—if you can even pay in the afterlife. Some ghosts got so bored they started online shopping. See why delivery times are... tricky.

🐇 The Deep Warren

A whimsical, slightly unhinged dimension where logic bends. Rabbits are the size of horses, clocks run backwards, and everything has a magical twist. It's adorable, creepy, and entirely unpredictable.

🌃 The City of Umbra

The beating heart where ALL realms intersect. A sprawling metropolis where vampires walk beside werewolves, witches bargain with ghosts, and cryptids from everywhere come to... shop. The Eerie Market is located here—a chaotic bazaar of the bizarre, the cursed, and the covetable.

🦇 OUR CHARACTERS

🧛 Bernard – QA Supervisor

Age: 800+ years | Origin: The Crimson Courts

Bernard used to be a noble vampire, but got "bored with the politics." Now he works as Quality Assurance Supervisor for The Eerie Market. He reviews products with the weary exhaustion of someone who's seen it all. His catchphrase: "I've seen better curses in the 15th century."

🧛‍♂️ Valerus – Antique Shop Owner

Age: 847 years | Origin: The Crimson Courts

The ultimate snob. Valerus has "pure blood" superiority complex and thinks everything after 1800 is "tacky." His shop, Cursed Curiosities & Co., sells only the finest haunted antiques—most of which have killed at least three owners. He judges everyone silently.

🐺 The Werewolf – Master Groomer

Origin: The Howling Moors

Working at Monster Mane Makeovers, they're the best beast barber in the realms. Need your fur styled for a full moon? Shedding survival kit? They got you. Customers leave either beautifully groomed... or in one piece. Both acceptable.

🦇 Batty Byte – Punk Influencer

Origin: The Deep Warren (sort of)

This small but fierce bat has more Instagram followers than most monsters have centuries. She reviews products with enthusiasm that borders on concerning, and her tagline "KA-POW! GLAMOUR!" is known throughout Umbra. She once reviewed a cursed lipstick and it made her fangs sparkle for a week.

🧙‍♀️ The Witch – Witchy Haul

Origin: The Witch's Wilds

Every witch needs supplies, and this one NEVER stops shopping. Her "Witchy Haul" series is legendary—she's purchased so many cauldrons, familiar treats, and hex ingredients that the shipping empire had to expand three realms. Her cart is always overflowing.

🛍️ THE EERIE MARKET

Located in the City of Umbra, The Eerie Market is the one place in all the realms where monsters can buy goods from each other—safely. Think of it as Etsy meets Amazon... if Amazon was run by things that go bump in the night.
