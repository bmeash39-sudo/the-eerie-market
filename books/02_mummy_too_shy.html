🧻 The Mummy Who Was Too Shy 🧻

"A Story About Finding Courage Outside Your Comfort Zone"

[Illustration: A cute worried mummy with big eyes]

A The Eerie Market Original Story

"Good heavens! Just TRY!"

Once upon a time, in a dusty old tomb, there lived a little mummy named Mo.

Mo was wrapped in long white bandages from head to toe. But there was one problem — she was SO shy!

"Someone might see my wrappings!" she said. "What if they look at me WRONG?"

She stayed hidden in her tomb for 5,000 years.

[Illustration: Mo hiding behind a tombstone]

2

One day, a brave little bat flew into the tomb. "Why are you hiding?" asked Batty Byte.

"I'm too shy to come out," whispered Mo. "What if someone sees my bandages aren't PERFECT? What if they think I'm NOT enough?"

Batty smiled. "You don't have to be perfect. Just be YOU. The world isn't as scary as you think!"

3

Mo took a tiny step outside. The sun was warm. The breeze was gentle.

She gasped! "The world... it's not so scary after all!"

She took another step. And another.

And for the first time in 5,000 years, Mo realized something important:

Being shy is okay — but trying new things is even better!
