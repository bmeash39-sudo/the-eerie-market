🦇 Batty Byte's Big Dream 🦇

"A Story About Dreaming Big and Going For It!"

[Illustration: A punk rock bat with pink and black hair]

"KA-POW! GLAMOUR!"

Born in the quiet corners of the Deep Warren, Batty Byte was a sheltered country bat.

She had wings but barely used them. Her world was small, safe, and... boring.

She always wanted MORE.

One night, she spread her wings and flew toward the glowing City of Umbra!

[Illustration: Batty flying toward city lights]

She stayed hidden, watching monsters go about their lives, until she saw something MAGICAL:

A monster was applying a colored tube to their lips. The color sparkled. The monster smiled.

Batty's heart raced. She WANTED that tube. She NEEDED that tube.

She dove down, grabbed the fallen lipstick, applied it to her tiny fangs — and something UNEXPECTED happened.

Her fangs glittered. They GLOWED!

The video she accidentally recorded went viral overnight.

10,000 followers! Comments galore! Everyone wanted to see the glitter-bat!

Now she's got a tagline that echoes through Umbra:

"KA-POW! GLAMOUR!"

And she lived happily ever after — being HERSELF, loud and proud!

The End 🦇

"Dream big. Be loud. KA-POW!"
