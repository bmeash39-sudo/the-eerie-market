🦉 Origin Stories – The Eerie Market

🧛 Bernard — "The Bore-geois"

Born into the Crimson Courts, Bernard was destined to be a terrifying vampire. But he was… bored. After centuries of endless blood‑sucking and meetings, he quit the court and walked into the mortal world, landing a job as Quality Assurance Supervisor at The Eerie Market. Now he reviews monsters’ products with the dead‑pan line, “I’ve seen better curses in the 15th century.”

🧛‍♂️ Valerus — "The Spoiled Aristocrat"

847 years of old‑money has turned Valerus into a snob who only appreciates antique relics from before the 18th century. He opened "Cursed Curiosities & Co." not for profit, but to prove everyone else’s taste is trash. He’s superior, unforgiving, yet secretly helpful—though he’ll never admit it.

🐺 The Werewolf — "The Stray Who Made Good"

Found wandering the Howling Moors, the Werewolf discovered a hidden talent for grooming. He turned his claws into clippers and now runs Monster Mane Makeovers, the premier beast barber in all six realms. Some customers leave looking fabulous; others leave in one piece—both count as success.

🧙‍♀️ The Witch — "The Hoarder Who Found Purpose"

The Witch has always loved shopping. From mushroom barter as a child to hex‑infused hauls as an adult, her cart never empties. Her “Witchy Haul” series livestreams her latest cursed finds, making her a legend in the monster community while she lives her best hex‑filled life.

🧻 The Mummy — "The Wrapped and The Wronged"

Wrapped for 5,000 years, the Mummy never knew a life beyond bandages. Now she roams, horrified at any glimpse of exposed skin. She constantly clutches her wrappings, proclaiming “GOOD HEAVENS!” to keep modesty alive in the undead world.

🧟 The Zombie — "The Reformed Undead"

Once an ordinary office worker, he was dramatically re‑killed and rose as a zombie. Tired of brain cravings, he turned to veganism, clutching wilted lettuce like it owes him money. He’s on a slow, leafy path of self‑improvement—one kale leaf at a time.

🦇 Batty Byte — "The Country Bat Who Went Big"

Born in the quiet Deep Warren, Batty Byte was a sheltered country bat dreaming of more. She fled to the towering Monster City, hid in shadows, and saw a monster applying a colored tube to their lips. She snatched the tube, painted her tiny fangs, and the glittering result went viral. Now she’s a 10K‑follower influencer shouting ‘KA‑POW! GLAMOUR!’ across the realms.

👻 Ghost Girl — "The Ethereal Dreamer"

A kawaii ghost girl, forever floating in a medieval dress, reaches for a lone candelabra. She drifts through haunted halls, leaving gentle orbs of light. Her soft smile and glowing heart make her the perfect mascot for The Faded Veil, reminding monsters that even spirits can be cute.
